Something about me!


I come from a small village situated among meadows and forests. Since I was a child, I admired my mother's baked goods and wanted to bake a wonderful cake. I baked my first cake when I was 5, of course with my mother's help. It was a cake for my beloved grandmother's birthday. I still remember the delight and joy in her eyes. Since then, I decided to bake wonderful cakes to make people celebrate something special and happy.

My cakes are special!


What makes my cakes special? Properly selected ingredients: we only use natural, organic ingredients from well-known suppliers. I try to buy from local farmers so I can always be sure that everything is fresh and the cake will be exceptionally delicious.
